## Full Description (4000 chars max)
How far can you run?

Bus Back is a heart-pounding endless runner where you're behind the wheel, weaving through traffic, dodging obstacles, and staying one step ahead of relentless pursuit vehicles — all while the city blurs past at breakneck speed.

FEATURES
• Fluid 3D gameplay — Temple Run-style behind-the-car perspective
• 3-lane system with instant switches, jumps, and ducks
• 5 obstacle types to dodge: road hazards, debris, vehicles, cones, and barriers
• Pursuit vehicles that close the gap as your meter fills
• Shootout phase — tap targets to break free when cornered
• Score by distance — how far can you get?
• Synthesized audio engine that responds to your speed
• Works fully offline — no internet required
• Free to play, no ads during gameplay

HOW TO PLAY
← → Switch lanes  |  ↑ Jump  |  ↓ Duck
Swipe on mobile for all controls

THE PURSUIT METER
Hit obstacles and the pursuit meter climbs. Let it hit 100% and the Shootout begins — tap all targets to break free. Miss too many and it's game over.

Built for mobile and desktop. No account needed. Just drive.
